Privacy Policy for Hackathon Registration

Effective Date: June 7, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how C.D. Tech ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the email addresses provided by participants for the Gig City Data Jam hackathon. By registering for the event, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

We collect your email address when you register for the hackathon. This information is necessary for:

  • Acting as a headcount approximation for the event organizer.
  • Communicating important event details and updates.
  • Sending pre-event and post-event materials.

Use of Information

Your email address will be used for:

  • Event-related communication: Updates, schedules, and other essential information.
  • Post-event follow-up: Feedback surveys, announcements, and community engagement opportunities.
  • Inclusion in C.D. Tech's mailing list for future events and news, unless you opt-out.

Information Storage and Security

The collected email addresses will be securely stored in an Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 bucket.

The S3 bucket will be destroyed, and all email addresses will be deleted after the event, except for the organizer's retention of the email list for ongoing communications.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
